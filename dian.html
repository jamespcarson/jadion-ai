Co-Founder and Managing Partner

Dian Xu

Data Platform Architecture Enterprise AI Data Governance PSYCH-K Advanced MA Economics

Dian Xu is a senior data and technology executive, strategist, and trusted advisor with a proven track record of leading enterprise transformation through data, analytics, and AI. As the Co-Founder of Jadion LLC, she helps organizations turn complex data challenges into scalable business capabilities, aligning strategy, platform modernization, analytics, and leadership to create measurable impact.

With more than a decade of executive experience leading data organizations at scale, Dian has built and modernized enterprise data platforms, led high-performing engineering and analytics teams, and helped shape customer, retention, and growth strategies powered by data. Her work sits at the intersection of business vision and technical execution: translating ambiguity into clarity, building trust across functions, and designing practical roadmaps that move organizations from fragmented data environments to modern, AI-ready ecosystems.

Dian is known for combining deep analytical rigor with a sharp understanding of how organizations actually operate. She brings a rare ability to connect executive priorities with platform architecture, operational realities, and long-term transformation goals. Her leadership spans enterprise data strategy, cloud modernization, data platform design, business intelligence, analytics operating models, AI enablement, and organizational capability building. Whether advising on a modernization roadmap, standing up scalable data foundations, or helping leaders unlock more value from their data investments, Dian focuses on solutions that are not only technically sound, but adopted, trusted, and built to last.

As a first-generation immigrant and business leader, Dian's perspective is grounded in resilience, adaptability, and service. She believes the best data strategies are not just about technology, they are about empowering people, improving decisions, and unlocking what is possible for organizations willing to transform. Her leadership style is both strategic and deeply human: she is passionate about building strong teams, cultivating future leaders, and creating environments where truth, curiosity, and accountability can thrive together.

Dian holds a Master's degree in Economics from Eastern Michigan University. Her foundation in economics continues to shape her approach to consulting today: rigorous in thought, disciplined in analysis, and always focused on helping clients see the story behind the numbers. Through Jadion LLC, she brings that same mindset to every engagement, partnering with leaders who are ready to modernize boldly, lead clearly, and build enduring advantage through data.
